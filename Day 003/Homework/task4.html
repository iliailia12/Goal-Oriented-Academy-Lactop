Fourth planet from the Sun

Σαν σήμερα: Το Mariner 9 φτάνει στον Άρη Fourth planet from the Sun
Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is known for its reddish appearance due to iron oxide on its surface. It is a cold,
dry planet that was once warmer and wetter, with evidence suggesting it had a thicker
atmosphere capable of supporting flowing water billions
of years ago. Mars is home to the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons, which is about 21 km high, and has
two moons, Phobos and Deimos. It has been extensively explored by various missions,
including rovers that have provided valuable data about its geology and climate. NASA+3
